How it works

Everything you need is already around you

1
Tell us what you can share
Pick categories of things you'd be happy to lend — tools, kitchen gear, games, garden equipment. Takes 60 seconds and you're done.
2
Post what you need
When you need something, post a quick request. Tell us what it is, when you need it, and any details that help a neighbor know if their item fits.
3
The right neighbors are notified
We alert only the neighbors most likely to have what you need — based on what they can share and how close they are. No spam. No noise.
4
Share, return, repeat
Meet your neighbor, borrow the item, return it when you're done. The more your neighborhood shares, the stronger it becomes.
